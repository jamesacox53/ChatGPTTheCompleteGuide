Midjourney lets you easily turn images into a certain art style.

example prompts:
/imagine a painting of a young woman wearing sunglasses, portrait,
pop-art style

/imagine a painting of a young woman wearing sunglasses, portrait,
cubism style

you can also ask for images in the style of a certain artist.

/imagine a painting of three young people walking,
panorama, van-gogh style, park in the background

/imagine a painting of a man, close-up, johannes vermeer style
