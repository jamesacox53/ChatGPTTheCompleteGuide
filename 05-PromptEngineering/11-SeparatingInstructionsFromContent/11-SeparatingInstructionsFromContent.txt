Sometimes ChatGPT can get confused when you provide an article. It can get confused
between the instructions and the article. So you can wrap the article in """ or ---
to make ChatGPT know that this is the article and everything else is the instructions.
For example:

Summarize the below article article:
"""
...
"""

Summarize the above article, starting like this:
The key takeaways of the article are:
-
